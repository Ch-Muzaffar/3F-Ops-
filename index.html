  • Multi-step conditional forms that adapt to each customer's input
  • Automated WhatsApp and email confirmation dispatched in under 3 seconds

03 — Allocation & Retention

Real-Time Grid Allocation & Retention Engines

Live operational dashboards that replace guesswork with certainty — so your resources are always allocated, your revenue is always secured, and your no-show rate approaches zero.

A real-time visual grid that gives you and your staff a live view of every allocation — slots, tables, bays, rooms, appointments — with instant lock-in on partial deposits.

  • Color-coded live grid updating in real-time across all staff devices
  • Automatic hold-and-release logic with configurable deposit thresholds
  • Cancellation penalty logic to create real financial commitment
  • SMS/WhatsApp reminders auto-dispatched at configurable intervals

Built on WebSocket-powered real-time state management, backed by PostgreSQL row-level locking to prevent double-allocation — even during simultaneous high-traffic booking windows.

  • Socket.io or Supabase Realtime for live state synchronization
  • Stripe or JazzCash deposit collection built in
  • Mobile-optimized staff dashboard with offline-fallback caching
  • Custom retention rule engine configurable per business type

Our allocation and retention systems directly attack two of the most expensive problems in service businesses: empty slots due to no-shows, and chaotic resource scheduling.

  • No-show rate reduction: down to 4.8% from industry average of 22%
  • Deposit collection compliance: 93.1% across active deployments
  • Staff scheduling conflicts reduced to near-zero within first week
  • Revenue recovery from prevented no-shows averages PKR 47,300/month
